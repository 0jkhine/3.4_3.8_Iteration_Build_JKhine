History of music

Music has evolved since the start of civilization, from court bards to indie bands and independent artists to studios, music has changed throughout the years immensely with many new genre's being made daily.

Music has been used as a form of expression, art form, a outlet for creativity, and a tradition. Production of music has changed so much from making noises that sound good together to music sheets and tablature to full DAWs (Digital Audio Workstation) in use with audio interfaces and midi devices, all being affordable by the common producer.

There is already AI capable of creating music from just text prompts available to the public for free. Music will forever be a core feature of human culture, and will continue to evolve and expand.
